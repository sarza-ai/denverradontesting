Testing Methods

The right tool for every property

We use two EPA-accepted testing methodologies, chosen based on property type, timeline, and the most accurate results for your situation.

Continuous Radon Monitors

Recommended for Real Estate

Our RADStar RS800 continuous electronic monitors are the most advanced short-term testing devices available. They record hourly radon readings throughout the test period and produce a tamper-evident report — giving buyers, sellers, and agents complete confidence in the results.

  • Results available same day after pickup
  • Tamper-evident, hourly data logging
  • NRPP & EPA protocol compliant
  • Ideal for buyers, sellers & inspectors
  • Digital report delivered via email

Liquid Scintillation Vials

Large & Multi-Unit Properties

For schools, daycares, apartment buildings, and large commercial properties that require testing across multiple zones, liquid scintillation vials offer greater accuracy and scalability than traditional charcoal canisters. We deploy and retrieve vials at multiple points throughout the property.

  • More accurate than charcoal alternatives
  • Scalable to any number of test zones
  • Schools, daycares & apartment complexes
  • Laboratory-analyzed for precision
  • Comprehensive multi-zone report
The Process

Testing in 4 simple steps

From scheduling to final report, the entire process is designed to be fast and friction-free.

1

Schedule

Call, text, or fill out our form. We'll confirm a deployment time that works for your schedule — often same or next day.

2

Deploy

We place certified monitors at lowest livable level per EPA guidelines. The 48-hour test runs unattended.

3

Retrieve

After the test period, we collect the equipment and process the data on-site or at our lab.

4

Report

Receive a professional, NRPP-certified report via email — same day pickup for continuous monitors.
